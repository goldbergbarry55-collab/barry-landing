Who Is Barry?

I'M BARRY.
I MAKE PSYCHOLOGY
PERSONAL.

I help people aged 25–35 understand the emotional patterns quietly running their lives — anxiety, overthinking, burnout, people-pleasing, and more.

I don't give advice. I explain why you feel the way you feel. When you understand the pattern, you can break it.

For the past 7 years, I've been fascinated by how our childhood shapes our adult relationships, work, and sense of self. My own journey through anxiety and burnout led me to psychology — not as a degree, but as a tool for understanding myself and others.

Every reel I post is grounded in real psychology research, lived experience, and the belief that you don't need to feel broken to seek understanding. You just need to be willing to look.
